Synthetic Dataset Generation of Driver Telematics

@article{So2021SyntheticDG,

The techniques employed in the production of a synthetic dataset of driver telematics emulated from a similar real insurance dataset, aimed to produce a resource that can be used to advance models to assess risks for usage-based insurance, are described.
